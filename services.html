Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our platform and services

FinergyCloud is an AI-driven risk intelligence platform specifically designed for renewable energy investors. Our platform combines advanced IRR simulation, ESG scoring, and AI-powered risk profiling to help investors make smarter decisions in emerging market renewable energy projects. The current MVP includes working financial models, and we're developing advanced AI capabilities for 2025.

Our XGBoost machine learning model achieves 87% accuracy in predicting project success and IRR for renewable energy projects in emerging markets. The model has been validated with real-world projects and continues to improve as we incorporate more data. Key performance metrics include a 92% AUC score and the ability to predict IRR within ±1.5% for most projects.

FinergyCloud supports a wide range of renewable energy projects, including solar PV, wind, hydro, biomass, and geothermal. Our platform is designed to handle the unique characteristics of each technology type, with specialized features and risk models tailored to each. We currently have the most extensive data and experience with solar and wind projects in emerging markets.

Our ESG scoring framework evaluates projects across environmental, social, and governance dimensions using a comprehensive set of metrics tailored to renewable energy. The framework incorporates international standards and local context factors to provide a holistic assessment. Scores range from 1-10, with detailed breakdowns for each component and actionable recommendations for improvement.
