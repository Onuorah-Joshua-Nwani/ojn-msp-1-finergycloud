What We Offer

FinergyCloud empowers clean energy investors and developers with intelligent digital tools to assess ESG alignment, risk exposure, and project readiness for funding.

🧠 AI Risk Profiling

Assess location-based risk, technology uncertainty, and market conditions using intelligent project filters.

📈 IRR Simulation Tool

Estimate internal rate of return (IRR) based on solar or hybrid system inputs, ideal for early-stage project screening.

🌿 ESG Alignment Scoring

Automatically evaluate environmental, social, and governance indicators aligned with global sustainability frameworks.

🔍 Investor Match Engine

(Coming soon) Match project developers with interested ESG-aligned investors based on geography and risk appetite.

📄 Due Diligence Reports

Export automated summaries of project metrics, financial forecasts, and ESG scoring for funding discussions or pitches.
