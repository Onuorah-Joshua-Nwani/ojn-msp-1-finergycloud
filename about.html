Our Story

Originating as a university MBA dissertation, FinergyCloud evolved into a live MVP, registered company, and an open-source-led innovation journey. We saw a mismatch between what African and UK-based SMEs need vs what traditional banking and ERP systems offer.

Our founder combined fintech expertise with cloud-native tools to build an agile platform that helps businesses grow without financial complexity.
