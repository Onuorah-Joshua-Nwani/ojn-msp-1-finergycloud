Our Backstory

FinergyCloud was conceived during a Cardiff Metropolitan University MBA program in 2022, driven by a desire to address inefficiencies in Africa’s financial infrastructure. With research focused on digital transformation and ESG compliance, the company was incorporated in the UK to build scalable, ethical financial technology.

Our Journey

  • 2022: Idea conceived from MBA research on digital finance.
  • 2023: MVP built and launched on GitHub Pages.
  • 2024: Incorporated in the UK, branding and domain launched.
  • 2025: Applying to UK accelerators and Global Talent Visa.
