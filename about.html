Our Vision

FinergyCloud was founded with one goal: to revolutionize the financial cloud landscape. With over two decades of fintech experience, we identified a critical gap between available technology and real-world business needs—particularly in accounting, analytics, banking, and compliance.

Our mission is simple: To simplify, secure, and supercharge financial cloud solutions—no matter the business size.

We are building a smarter, faster, and more compliant cloud ecosystem for finance teams across the globe.

Key Milestones

  • 2018: Company founded with a focus on cloud fintech in emerging markets.
  • 2019: Onboarded first financial institution and launched internal alpha version.
  • 2020: Recognized at Global Fintech Awards and reached 500 customers.
  • 2021: Expanded to serve clients across 3 continents.
  • 2022: Rolled out AI-driven forecasting and ESG-focused modules.
  • 2023–Present: Scaling our MVP and joining ecosystem accelerators.
