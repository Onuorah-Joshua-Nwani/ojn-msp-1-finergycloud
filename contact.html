Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about FinergyCloud

FinergyCloud is an AI-driven platform that provides risk intelligence for renewable energy investments. It combines IRR simulation, ESG scoring, and AI-powered risk profiling to help investors make data-driven decisions about renewable energy projects, particularly in emerging markets. Our platform transforms complex financial modeling into intuitive, actionable insights.

Yes! Our MVP is live on GitHub and available for immediate testing. It includes core features like IRR calculation, risk assessment, and ESG scoring. You can access it through our GitHub repository or request a personalized demo through this contact form. We're actively seeking feedback from beta users to improve the platform.

FinergyCloud specifically focuses on renewable energy investments in emerging markets, integrating ESG factors from the ground up. Our AI-first approach, combined with emerging market expertise and transparent algorithms, sets us apart from generic investment analysis tools. We understand the unique challenges of renewable energy projects in developing economies.

Our roadmap includes full AI engine integration with XGBoost models and NLP capabilities planned for 2025. We're currently in the MVP phase with rule-based systems, and we're actively seeking partnerships and funding to accelerate development. Early adopters will get priority access to new features as they're released.

We offer flexible pricing tiers from individual investors ($99/month) to enterprise solutions (custom pricing). All plans include a 14-day free trial with no setup fees. During our beta phase, we're offering special pricing for early adopters and strategic partners. Contact us for detailed pricing information tailored to your specific needs.
