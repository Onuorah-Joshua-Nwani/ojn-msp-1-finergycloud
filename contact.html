Quick answers to common questions about FinergyCloud

FinergyCloud is an AI-driven risk intelligence platform specifically designed for renewable energy investors. Our platform combines advanced IRR simulation, ESG scoring, and AI-powered risk profiling to help investors make smarter decisions in emerging market renewable energy projects. The current MVP includes working financial models, and we're developing advanced AI capabilities for 2025.

Absolutely! Our MVP is live and accessible on GitHub for anyone to explore. We also offer personalized demos where we can show you how the platform works with your specific use cases. FinergyCloud is uniquely focused on renewable energy investments in emerging markets, with AI-first architecture and built-in ESG integration. Unlike generic financial tools, we understand the specific challenges of renewable energy projects in developing economies. Our platform is built on academic research (MBA distinction) and combines sophisticated modeling with user-friendly interfaces.
