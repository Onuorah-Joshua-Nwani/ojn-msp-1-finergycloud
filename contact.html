FinergyCloud is an AI-driven platform that provides risk intelligence for renewable energy investments. It combines IRR simulation, ESG scoring, and AI-powered risk profiling to help investors make data-driven decisions about renewable energy projects, particularly in emerging markets.

Yes! Our MVP is live on GitHub and available for testing. It includes core features like IRR calculation, risk assessment, and ESG scoring. You can access it through our GitHub repository or request a personalized demo through this contact form.

FinergyCloud specifically focuses on renewable energy investments in emerging markets, integrating ESG factors from the ground up. Our AI-first approach, combined with emerging market expertise and transparent algorithms, sets us apart from generic investment analysis tools.
