1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Create an account or register
  • Complete forms in the app
  • Upload project data for analysis
  • Contact our support team
  • Subscribe to notifications

This information may include:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials
  • Professional information
  • Financial information related to renewable energy projects

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our mobile app, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Performance data (crashes, errors)
  • Mobile analytics data

1.3 Mobile Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access (to connect to our services)
  • Storage access (to save calculations and reports)
  • Camera (optional, for document scanning)
  • Notifications (for alerts and updates)

You can manage these permissions through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

2.1 Provide and Improve Our Services

  • Deliver mobile app features and functionality
  • Process and analyze renewable energy project data
  • Generate risk assessments and financial simulations
  • Improve and enhance the mobile experience
  • Develop new mobile features

2.2 Communication and Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Provide information about app updates
  • Send mobile-specific newsletters (with your consent)

2.3 Analytics and Performance

  • Analyze mobile app usage patterns
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Generate aggregated, anonymized insights
  • Improve our machine learning models

2.4 Security and Compliance

  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Verify your identity on mobile devices
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our mobile app, including:

  • Mobile app hosting providers
  • Mobile analytics services
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors for in-app purchases

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Protect our rights or property
  • Protect user safety or public safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • Respond to legal process or government request

3.3 Business Transfers

If FinergyCloud is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your consent to do so.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information on our mobile app:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments of our mobile app
  • Secure development practices

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4.1 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4.2 Offline Storage

Our mobile app may store some data locally on your device for offline access. This data is protected using device-level encryption when available.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can access most of your personal information directly through the mobile app. You may also request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

5.2 Correction

You can update most of your personal information directly in the app settings. For information you cannot update yourself, contact us for assistance.

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data through the app settings or by contacting us. Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

5.4 Marketing Preferences

You can opt out of marketing communications by:

  • Adjusting notification settings in the app
  • Following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails
  • Contacting us directly

5.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please:

  • Use the relevant features in the app settings
  • Contact us at privacy@finergycloud.com

6. Mobile-Specific Privacy

6.1 App Permissions

Our mobile app may request certain permissions to function properly. You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

6.2 Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to your device. You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or your device settings.

6.3 Mobile Analytics

We use mobile analytics services to understand how users interact with our app. These services may collect information such as how often you use the app, events that occur within the app, and performance data.

6.4 Offline Functionality

Our app provides offline functionality that may store data locally on your device. This data is synchronized with our servers when your device is online.

6.5 App Stores

When you download our mobile app, you may also provide information to the app store (such as Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not control the data collection by app stores, and their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending an in-app notification
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the app after the revised Privacy Policy indicates your acceptance of the changes.
